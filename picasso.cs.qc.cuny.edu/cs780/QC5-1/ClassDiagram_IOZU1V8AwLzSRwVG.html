Class Take

Documentation:
Represents the ternary relation Take(Student, CourseSection, Grade).
Take(s, c, g) means student s took course section c and received grade g, or s is currently taking c with grade g undetermined.
Association student(1) to Student
Association grade(1) to Grade
Association courseSection(1) to CourseSection

Class Employment

Documentation:
Represents the ternary relation Employment(Employee, Office, JobDescription).
Employment(e, o, j) means employee e worked in the past, or is currently working, for office o with job description j.
Association jobDescription(1) to JobDescription
Association employee(1) to Employee
Association office(1) to Office
