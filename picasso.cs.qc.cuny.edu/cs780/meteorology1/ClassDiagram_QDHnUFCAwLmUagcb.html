Class Front

Generalization Hierarchy
Graph
| +-Line
| +-OpenLine
| +-AtmosphericObject
| +-Front
Documentation:
The direction of each front is recorded by selecting one of its two end nodes (stored by the "endNode" relation) and indicating if the direction is to the left or right of the line viewed from the selected end node. For warm, cold, and occluded fronts, the stored directions indicate the directions of their movement. For stationary fronts, the stored directions indicate the directions of warm air mass.
Super Class
OpenLine, AtmosphericObject
Subclasses
Cold, Warm, Stationary, Occluded
Association endNode(1) to OpenLineNode

Attribute Summary
Unspecified chardirection

Attribute Detail

Unspecified char direction

Documentation:
The values of 'L' (left) and 'R' (right) are used to indicate the front's direction viewed from the selected end node.
